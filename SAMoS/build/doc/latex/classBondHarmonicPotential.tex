\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Bond\+Harmonic\+Potential}{p.}{classBondHarmonicPotential} implements standard harmonic bond potential \doxyref{Potential}{p.}{classPotential} is given as $ U_{harm}\left(r_{ij}\right) = \frac{1}{2}k\left(\left|\vec r_{ij}\right|-l_0\right)^2 $, where $ k $ is the spring constant, $ l_0 $ is the the rest length and $ \vec r_{ij} = \vec r_i - \vec r_j $.
