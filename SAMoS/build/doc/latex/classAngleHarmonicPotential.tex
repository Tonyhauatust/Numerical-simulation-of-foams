\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Angle\+Harmonic\+Potential}{p.}{classAngleHarmonicPotential} implements standard harmonic angle potential \doxyref{Potential}{p.}{classPotential} is given as $ U_{harm}\left(\theta\right) = k_\theta\left(\theta-\theta_0\right)^2 $, where $ k_\theta $ is the stiffness, $ \theta $ is the angle between two bonds originating from the particle and $ \theta_0 $ is the rest (equilibrium) value of the angle. Note that we absorbed usual prefactor of $ 1/2 $ into the definition of $ k_\theta $.
