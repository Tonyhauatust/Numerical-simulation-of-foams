\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pair\+Motor\+Potential}{p.}{classPairMotorPotential} is a rather crude attempt to model effects of molecular motors acting between two filaments (e.\+g., actin cables) in a particle based model. The idea here is to introduce an additional force that acts on a pair of particles if their directors ( $ \vec n $), roughly speaking, point in opposite directions. More precisely, for a pair of particles $ i $ and $ j $ within distance $ a $, if $ \zeta = \vec n_i \cdot \vec n_j < 0 $, each particle receives a force of magnitude $ \alpha |\zeta| $. Force on each particle points in the direction of the corresponding director. We note that this is quite artificial as it introduces motion of the centre of mass of the pair. ~\newline
