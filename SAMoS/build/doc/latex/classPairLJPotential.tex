\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pair\+L\+J\+Potential}{p.}{classPairLJPotential} implements standard Lennard Jones potential $ U_{LJ}\left(r_{ij}\right) = 4\varepsilon \left[\left(\frac \sigma r_{ij}\right)^{12}-\left(\frac \sigma r_{ij}\right)^6)\right] $, where $ \varepsilon $ is the potential strength, $ \sigma $ is the particle diameter and $ r_{ij} $ is the interparticle distance.
