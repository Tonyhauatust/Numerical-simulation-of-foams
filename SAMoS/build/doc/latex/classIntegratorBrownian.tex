{\footnotesize\ttfamily void integrate (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}{ }\end{DoxyParamCaption})\hspace{0.3cm}{\ttfamily [virtual]}}



Propagate system for a time step. 

This function integrates equations of motion as introduced in the Eqs. (1a) and (1b) of Y. Fily, et al., ar\+Xiv\+:1309.\+3714 $ \partial_t \vec r_i = v_0 \hat{\vec n_i} + \mu \sum_{i\neq j}\vec F_{ij} $ and $ \partial_t \vartheta_i = \eta_i(t) $, where $ \mu $ is the mobility, $ \vartheta_i $ defines orientation of the velocity in the tangent plane, $ \hat{\vec n}_i = \left(\cos\vartheta_i,\sin\vartheta_i\right) $, and $ \eta_i(t) $ is the Gaussian white noise with zero mean and delta function correlations, $ \left<\eta_i(t)\eta_j(t\textnormal{\textquotesingle})\right> = 2\nu_r\delta_{ij}\delta\left(t-t\textnormal{\textquotesingle}\right) $, with $ \nu_r $ being the rotational diffusion rate.
