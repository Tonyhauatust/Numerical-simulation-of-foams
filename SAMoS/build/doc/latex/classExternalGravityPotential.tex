\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{External\+Gravity\+Potential}{p.}{classExternalGravityPotential} handles external gravitational potential that is for simplicity assumed to act in the z-\/direction (downwards). In other words, $ V_{grav}\left(r\right) = g z $ and the force is $ \vec F = - m g \vec e_z $, where $ g $ is gravitational acceleration.
