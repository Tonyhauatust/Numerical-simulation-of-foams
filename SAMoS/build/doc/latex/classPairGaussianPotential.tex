\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pair\+Gaussian\+Potential}{p.}{classPairGaussianPotential} implements a potential with two Gaussians shifted with respect to each other $ U_{Gauss}\left(r_{ij}\right) = A e^{-\alpha(r-r_A)^2} + B e^{-\beta(r-r_B)} $, where $ A $ and $ B $ control strengths of two parts, $ \alpha $ and $ \beta $ their widths, and $ r_A $ and $ r_b $ position of the Gaussian peaks.
