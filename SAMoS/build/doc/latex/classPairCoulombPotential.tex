\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pair\+Coulomb\+Potential}{p.}{classPairCoulombPotential} implements standard Coulomb potential with a LJ repulsive part to avoid diverging forces and interaction strengths for collapsing particles. \doxyref{Potential}{p.}{classPotential} is given as $ U_{Coul}\left(r_{ij}\right) = \frac{\alpha}{r_{ij}} + 4\left|\alpha\right|\left(\frac \sigma r_{ij}\right)^{12} $, where $ \alpha $ is the potential strength, $ \sigma $ is the particle diameter and $ r_{ij} $ is the interparticle distance.
