\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pair\+Nematic\+Align}{p.}{classPairNematicAlign} implements the \char`\"{}nematic\char`\"{} type alignment between neighbouring particles. For all particles within the cutoff distance $ r_{cut} \left(\leq r_{nl}\right) $ ( $ r_{nl} $ being neighbour list cutoff distance) we compute torque on the particle as $ \vec \tau_i = 2 J \sum_j \left(\vec n_i\cdot\vec n_j\right)\vec n_i\times\vec n_j $, where $ J $ is the coupling constant and $ \vec n_j $ is director of j-\/th neighbour.
