\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{External\+Harmonic\+Potential}{p.}{classExternalHarmonicPotential} handles external harmonic potential with repsect to a plane parallel to the xy-\/plane. In other words, $ V_{harm}\left(r\right) = \frac{1}{2} k (z-z0)^2 $ and the force is $ \vec F = - k (z - z0) \vec e_z $, where $ k $ is the stiffness constant and $ z_0 $ is the position of the plane along the z-\/axis.
