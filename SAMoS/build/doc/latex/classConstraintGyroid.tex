\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Enforces all particles to be on the surface of an approximate gyroid given by equation $ \cos \left(\frac{2 \pi x}{lx}\right) \sin \left(\frac{2 \pi y}{ly}\right)+\sin \left(\frac{2 \pi x}{lx}\right) \cos \left(\frac{2 \pi z}{lz}\right)+\cos \left(\frac{2 \pi y}{ly}\right) \sin \left(\frac{2 \pi z}{lz}\right) = 0. $ All velocities will point in the tangent direction.

Computes normal to the surface. 

Compute normal to the surface at p

Normal vector is computed as the normalized gradient vector at point p. Coordinates of gradient vector are given as\+:

$ g_x = \frac{2 \pi \left(\cos \left(\frac{2 \pi x}{lx}\right) \cos \left(\frac{2 \pi z}{lz}\right)-\sin \left(\frac{2 \pi x}{lx}\right) \sin \left(\frac{2 \pi y}{ly}\right)\right)}{lx}, $ $ g_y = \frac{2 \pi \left(\cos \left(\frac{2 \pi x}{lx}\right) \cos \left(\frac{2 \pi y}{ly}\right)-\sin \left(\frac{2 \pi y}{ly}\right) \sin \left(\frac{2 \pi z}{lz}\right)\right)}{ly}, $ $ g_z = \frac{2 \pi \left(\cos \left(\frac{2 \pi y}{ly}\right) \cos \left(\frac{2 \pi z}{lz}\right)-\sin \left(\frac{2 \pi x}{lx}\right) \sin \left(\frac{2 \pi z}{lz}\right)\right)}{lz}. $
