\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pair\+Yukawa\+Potential}{p.}{classPairYukawaPotential} implements standard Yukawa potential. \doxyref{Potential}{p.}{classPotential} is given as $ U_{Yukawa}\left(r_{ij}\right) = g\frac{e^{-\kappa r_{ij}}}{r_{ij}} $, where $ g $ is the potential strength, $ \kappa $ is the inverse potentail range and $ r_{ij} $ is the interparticle distance.
