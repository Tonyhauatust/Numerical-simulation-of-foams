\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{External\+A\+J\+Polar\+Align}{p.}{classExternalAJPolarAlign} implements the active jamming type \char`\"{}polar\char`\"{} type alignment on a single particle. For all particles we compute torque on the particle as $ \vec \tau_i = -1/tau \vec n_i\times\vec v_i $, where $ tau $ is the coupling alignment time and $ \vec v_i $ is the velocity of the particle itself.
