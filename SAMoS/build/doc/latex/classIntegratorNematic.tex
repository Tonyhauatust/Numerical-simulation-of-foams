Propagate system for a time step. 

This function integrates equations of motion Eqs. (1a) and (1b) of Y. Fily, et al., ar\+Xiv\+:1309.\+3714 $ \partial_t \vec r_i = d_0 \kappa_i(t) \hat{\vec n_i} $ where $ d_0 $ is the elementary displacement and $ \kappa_i(t) $ is the instantaneous value of the random variable that can be either $ -1 $ of $ +1 $. and $ \partial_t \vartheta_i = \eta_i(t) $, where $ \mu $ is the mobility, $ \vartheta_i $ defines orientation of the velocity in the tangent plane, $ \hat{\vec n}_i = \left(\cos\vartheta_i,\sin\vartheta_i\right) $, and $ \eta_i(t) $ is the Gaussian white noise with zero mean and delta function correlations, $ \left<\eta_i(t)\eta_j(t\textnormal{\textquotesingle})\right> = 2\nu_r\delta_{ij}\delta\left(t-t\textnormal{\textquotesingle}\right) $, with $ \nu_r $ being the rotational diffusion rate.
