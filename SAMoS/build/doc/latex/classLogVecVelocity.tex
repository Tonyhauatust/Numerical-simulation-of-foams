\doxyref{Log\+Vec\+Velocity}{p.}{classLogVecVelocity} class. 

Logs magnitude of total velocity calculated as $ v = \frac{1}{N}\left|\sum_i \vec v_i\right| $. It is related to the Vicsek order parameters by dividing this quantity by $ v_0 $.
