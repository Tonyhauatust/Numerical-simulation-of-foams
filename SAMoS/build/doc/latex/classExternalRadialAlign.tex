\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{External\+Radial\+Align}{p.}{classExternalRadialAlign} implements the alignment to the external field that points radially towards a centre at attraction. This alignment is intended for wound healing simulations and we implicitly assume radial symmetry (i.\+e., that we have a planar system). Alignment filed is given as $ \vec H = J\vec e_r $, where $ \vec e_r $ is the radius vector pointing from the user-\/specified centre $ \vec r_c $ towards the particle $ \vec r_i $. In this case, torque is $ \vec \tau_i = \vec H \times \vec n_i $. User can specify that the centre of attraction follows the centre of mass.
