\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pair\+Velocity\+Align}{p.}{classPairVelocityAlign} implements the \char`\"{}velocity\char`\"{} type alignment between neighbouring particles. For all particles within the cutoff distance $ r_{cut} \left(\leq r_{nl}\right) $ ( $ r_{nl} $ being neighbour list cutoff distance) we compute torque on the particle as $ \vec \tau_i = 2 J \sum_j \left(\vec v_i\cdot\vec v_j\right)\vec v_i\times\vec v_j $ (for nematic coupling) and as $ \vec \tau_i = 2 J \sum_j \vec v_i\times\vec v_j $ (for polar case), where $ J $ is the coupling constant and $ \vec v_j $ is velocity of j-\/th neighbour.
