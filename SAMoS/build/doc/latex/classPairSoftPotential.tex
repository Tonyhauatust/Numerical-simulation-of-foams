\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Pair\+Soft\+Potential}{p.}{classPairSoftPotential} implements the soft repulsive force between particles. \doxyref{Potential}{p.}{classPotential} is given as $ U_{soft}\left(r_{ij}\right) = \frac{k}{2} \left(a_i + a_j - r_{ij}\right)^2 $, if $ r_{ij} \le a_i + a_j $ or $ U_{soft} = 0 $ if $ r_{ij} > a_i + a_j $. $ k $ is the potential strength, $ a_i $ and $ a_j $ are radii of the two particles and $ r_{ij} $ is the interparticle distance.
