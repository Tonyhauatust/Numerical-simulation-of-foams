PairMotorPotential Class Reference

Detailed Description

PairMotorPotential is a rather crude attempt to model effects of molecular motors acting between two filaments (e.g., actin cables) in a particle based model. The idea here is to introduce an additional force that acts on a pair of particles if their directors ( ), roughly speaking, point in opposite directions. More precisely, for a pair of particles and within distance , if , each particle receives a force of magnitude . Force on each particle points in the direction of the corresponding director. We note that this is quite artificial as it introduces motion of the centre of mass of the pair.
