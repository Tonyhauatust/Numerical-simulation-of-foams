BondActiveForce Class Reference

Detailed Description

BondActiveForce implements actie force along the chain. Force is along the tangent of the chain, which is in this case along the bond. It is where is the strength of the force and is the direction force is pointing in (i.e. along the bond connecting beads and .

Note
Both beads will get the same force (this is extrnal active force!) and the force on each bead will be sum of the forces acting on all bonds that are connected to it.
This class assumes that beads in consecutive bonds are ordered, i.e., that the chain has clearly defined head and the tail.
Potential would here make very little sense, so we do not define it.
